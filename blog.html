👋 About Me

I'm Shaila—a curious maker, crafter, closet maximalist and a poteto. This blog is a snapshot of all the things that make me tick, and things that i usually spend my time on: bold outfits, beautiful code, random stuffs, handmade chaos, and everyday wins that make life spark a little brighter.

🎨 Handcrafted Joy

Crochet. Fuxzzt wire. ribbons. Paper. Paint. I love making things with my hands that didn’t exist before. You’ll find DIY tutorials, behind-the-scenes project messes, and reflections on what handcrafting teaches us about patience, precision, and imperfection.

📚 Reading, Because Words Heal

Writing, for me, isn’t just craft—it’s connection. I journal to understand, blog to share, and occasionally scribble poetry that never leaves my drafts folder. This space is where I write from the heart, not just the grammar handbook.

🙏 Serving Jehovah God With Grace

Faith is a cornerstone of my life. Here, I’ll share devotionals, reflections, and honest thoughts about walking with God in a world that’s equal parts chaotic and beautiful. I'm not perfect, but I'm learning what grace really means.

🎬 Cinema Therapy

Movies are my escape, inspiration, and sometimes free therapy. You’ll find posts about films that made me cry, think, or laugh like a maniac—plus reflections on storytelling and character arcs that mirror real-life struggles and triumphs.
